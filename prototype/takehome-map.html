Prototype — educational estimate, not tax advice. Uses 2025 federal brackets, standard deduction, FICA / self-employment tax (with the Social Security wage-base coordinated across W-2 and 1099 income), additional Medicare tax, and the child tax credit with phase-out. State income tax uses simplified 2025 rate schedules applied to federal taxable income; it excludes local/city taxes (e.g. NYC, MD counties), state-specific deductions and credits, itemized deductions, and retirement contributions. Washington taxes only capital gains and New Hampshire only investment income, so both show as no wage tax.
